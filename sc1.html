Practice Mindfulness 🧘

Take a moment to breathe, relax, and reconnect with yourself.

Guided Breathing Exercise

Follow the circle — inhale, hold, exhale.

Press Start
4-7-8 Technique

📋 Instructions

  1. Find a quiet, comfortable place.
  2. Inhale through nose for 4 counts.
  3. Hold for 7 counts.
  4. Exhale through mouth for 8 counts.
  5. Repeat for several cycles.

💡 Tips

  • Practice for 5-10 minutes.
  • Gently refocus if mind wanders.
  • Use whenever you feel stressed.
  • Same time daily builds habit.

✨ Benefits

  • Reduces stress and anxiety.
  • Enhances concentration.
  • Promotes calm and relaxation.
  • Improves emotional resilience.

⏰ When to Practice

  • Morning for focus.
  • Work/study breaks to refresh.
  • Before bed for better sleep.
  • Anytime you're overwhelmed.
